Description

This lovely 2BDR apartment is located in Sintra, a charming Portuguese village set in the refreshing hills of the Serra de Sintra. Hidden in these pine-covered hills are extravagant palaces, extravagant villages, and the ruins of a Moorish castle. The variety of fascinating historic buildings and beautiful natural landscapes combine to form an exceptional tourist destination, and in our apartment, you will find comfort and all the amenities for an unforgettable stay.
