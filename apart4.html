Description

Welcome to Casa Solarenga, an idyllic countryside residence located just outside the picturesque city of Loulé - a charming city known for it's buzzing food market and historic old town. Casa Solarenga features 3 bedroom and 3 bathrooms combined with a spacious living area and kitchen. The Villa features multiple terraces and outdoor spaces overlooking the luscious hills of the Algarve, the landscaped private garden and the ocean in the far distance. The Villa features a private pool.
